4:T6d2,2019–2026 所有 MoE 论文都可以拆到三条轴上：routing（谁挑谁）、load balancing（怎么让专家别塌缩）、expert structure（专家多大、要不要 shared）。每条轴都发生过一次代际更替。Routing：Shazeer et al. [Shazeer2017Outrageous] 给出 top-k + aux loss 原型，Lepikhin et al. [Lepikhin2020GShard] 把它固化为生产模板（top-2 softmax + aux loss + capacity factor），Fedus et al. [Fedus2021Switch] 砍到 top-1，Zhou et al. [Zhou2022ExpertChoice] 反转为 expert-choose-token 以结构性消除不均衡，Wang et al. [Wang2024AuxFree] 再次反转：不换路由方向、只用 bias EMA 自我纠偏，最后被 DeepSeek-V3 [DeepSeek2024V3] 大规模验证。Load balance：GShard [Lepikhin2020GShard] 的 aux loss 永远在污染主 gradient；Zoph et al. [Zoph2022STMoE] 加 router z-loss 解决 logit overflow 这个正交问题；DeepSeek-V3 把 aux loss 彻底移除，只留 z-loss + bias EMA；Qiu et al. [Qiu2025LBLDemons] 指出即使用 aux loss，其实现细节（micro-batch vs global、是否 detach p、跨 DP 同步与否）常常比强度更重要——这反过来支持了 'bias EMA 更少坑位' 的工程判断。Expert structure：Shazeer / GShard / Switch / Mixtral [Shazeer2017Outrageous][Lepikhin2020GShard][Fedus2021Switch][Jiang2024Mixtral] 都是 8–64 大专家路线；Dai et al. [Dai2024DeepSeekMoE] 切到 64+ 并引入 1 个 shared expert，Nguyen et al. [Nguyen2025DSMoETheory] 从 identifiability 角度给出共享项的统计学解释。三条轴上的最新选择在 2024 下半年被 DeepSeek-V2/V3 [DeepSeekAI2024V2][DeepSeek2024V3] 打包为事实 default，FLAME-MoE 与 OpenMoE [Kang2025FLAMEMoE][Xue2024OpenMoE] 提供了可复现实证。5:T710,Every MoE paper from 2019–2026 decomposes into three axes: routing (who picks whom), load balancing (how to keep experts from collapsing), and expert structure (how big, shared or not). Each axis has gone through one generational turnover. Routing: Shazeer et al. [Shazeer2017Outrageous] introduced the top-k + aux-loss prototype; Lepikhin et al. [Lepikhin2020GShard] hardened it into the production template (top-2 softmax + aux loss + capacity factor); Fedus et al. [Fedus2021Switch] cut to top-1; Zhou et al. [Zhou2022ExpertChoice] flipped to expert-choose-token for structural balance; Wang et al. [Wang2024AuxFree] flipped again — same routing direction but bias-EMA self-correction — later validated at scale by DeepSeek-V3 [DeepSeek2024V3]. Load balancing: GShard's aux loss [Lepikhin2020GShard] perpetually pollutes the main gradient; Zoph et al. [Zoph2022STMoE] added router z-loss for the orthogonal logit-overflow issue; DeepSeek-V3 dropped aux loss entirely, keeping z-loss + bias EMA; Qiu et al. [Qiu2025LBLDemons] showed implementation details of aux loss (micro-batch vs global, detaching p, cross-DP sync) often dominate the coefficient — reinforcing the 'bias EMA has fewer footguns' engineering call. Expert structure: Shazeer / GShard / Switch / Mixtral [Shazeer2017Outrageous][Lepikhin2020GShard][Fedus2021Switch][Jiang2024Mixtral] all sit on the 8–64 coarse line; Dai et al. [Dai2024DeepSeekMoE] cut to 64+ and added 1 shared expert, with Nguyen et al. [Nguyen2025DSMoETheory] giving a statistical identifiability justification for the shared term. The latest choice on each axis was packaged as the de-facto default by DeepSeek-V2/V3 [DeepSeekAI2024V2][DeepSeek2024V3] in late 2024, with FLAME-MoE and OpenMoE [Kang2025FLAMEMoE][Xue2024OpenMoE] as reproducible instantiations.6:T5b7,Aux loss has two structural issues that no coefficient sweep can dodge. First, gradient pollution: aux-loss gradients on router logits mix with task gradients, preventing the 'routing decisions decoupled from model loss' property that bias EMA gives. Second, implementation fragility: once Qiu et al. [Qiu2025LBLDemons] decompose aux loss along micro-batch vs global, detach p or not, cross-DP sync or not, CV and specialization fluctuations routinely exceed the effect of sweeping the coefficient from 1e-2 to 1e-4. Bias EMA replaces this with a sign-gradient rule b_i ← b_i + η · sign(target − load_i): it edits only the bias, not router weights; η is dynamically tuned by dead-expert count; and dead-expert convergence has a stochastic-approximation proof [Han2025AuxFreeTheory]. DeepSeek-V3's [DeepSeek2024V3] published logs show token-drop rate <0.5% throughout and expert-usage CV stable at 0.12–0.18 — aux-loss runs at matched setup typically sit at CV≥0.25. Critical caveat: z-loss cannot be removed alongside aux loss. Z-loss controls router-logit norm (to prevent fp16 overflow), which is orthogonal to load balancing — hence DeepSeek-V3 keeps z-loss=1e-3 [Zoph2022STMoE]. Adjacent lines include Similarity-Preserving Routers [Omi2025SimilarityRouter] suppressing collapse via a structural router, and MoE++ [Jin2024MoEpp] adding zero-computation experts for adaptive compute — both on the 'balance as structural constraint' trajectory.7:T615,The template's victory doesn't mean MoE eats every scenario. Three boundaries deserve naming. (1) Post-training: router gradient signal thins out in SFT/RLHF, and multiple teams in 2024–2025 anecdotally report fine-grained MoE is less stable than dense here — but published matched-quality ablations remain thin; Chen et al. [Chen2022TaskPrune] and Koishekenov et al. [Koishekenov2022NLLBPrune] take the compromise path of 'MoE pretrain + task-specific expert pruning for deployment' and count as early empirical evidence for Camp D. (2) Upcycling: Liew et al. [Liew2025Upcycling] give a clean scaling-law boundary — the dense checkpoint must already be token-rich (typically past Chinchilla-optimal) for upcycling's effective-token factor to land in 0.4–0.6×; upcycling from token-poor dense is often net-negative. Szatkowski et al. [Szatkowski2023DenseToMoE] show an activation-sparsity-driven dense→MoE conversion path but at small scale. (3) Dense line: OLMo 2 / OLMo 3 [Walsh2024OLMo2][OLMo2025Olmo3] demonstrate via fully-open 7B/32B dense families that single-node serving, post-training stability, and reproducibility can matter more than MoE's quality-per-active-param for certain tasks — Camp B isn't denying MoE's scaling edge but pointing out dense still wins ROI at specific serving budgets. On the serving side MegaBlocks [Gale2022MegaBlocks], ExpertFlow [He2024ExpertFlow] and MoETuner [Go2025MoETuner] drive fine-grained MoE serving cost down, but single-node memory footprint remains the hard constraint MoE cannot fully cross.8:T825,一条可执行的清单（2026 Q2 版本）：

2. 已有 Mixtral-style coarse MoE：优先把专家拆细到 32–64 并加 1 个 shared expert；routing 可以暂不动。ablation 里 fine-grained 这一步的边际收益最高 [Dai2024DeepSeekMoE][Nguyen2025DSMoETheory]。
