[Kaplan2020ScalingLaws] argued in 2020 for 'bigger models, fewer tokens' and shaped the GPT-3 era. Two years later Hoffmann et al. [Hoffmann2022Chinchilla] used three independent fits — IsoFLOP, parametric loss, and learning-curve extrapolation — to converge on a tokens/param ≈ 20 compute-optimal ratio, retroactively labeling Gopher, PaLM and GPT-3 as under-trained; LLaMA [Touvron2023LLaMA] adopted the new prescription within a year. Muennighoff et al. [Muennighoff2023DataConstrained] extended the frontier into data-constrained regimes, Gadre et al. [Gadre2024OverTraining] and Bhagia et al. [Bhagia2024TaskLadders] decoupled loss-level scaling from per-task scaling, and Schaeffer et al. [Schaeffer2023Mirage] recast 'emergence' as a metric-choice artifact. This report does not re-narrate each curve. It answers a practical question for 2026: how should a serious pretraining team *use* scaling laws rather than *cite* them? The answer is to treat scaling-law fitting as an always-on sweep pipeline, not a set of three-decimal coefficients from someone else's paper.5:T741,2020 年 Kaplan et al. [Kaplan2020ScalingLaws] 在 WebText2 上给出 loss ~ N, D, C 的联合幂律，结论是在固定 compute 下应当把预算更多花在参数上——compute-optimal ratio 约 1.7 tokens/param。这套配方直接塑造了 GPT-3（175B / 300B tokens）与 Gopher [Rae2021Gopher]（280B / 300B tokens）。两年后 Hoffmann et al. [Hoffmann2022Chinchilla] 用三种互不依赖的方法（IsoFLOP 扫描、参数化损失拟合、学习曲线外推）一致收敛到 tokens/param ≈ 20，也就是说当时主流模型普遍 under-trained 一个数量级。Chinchilla-70B 训练 1.4T tokens 就击败 Gopher-280B 与 GPT-3-175B，是这条修正斜率最直接的物理证据。

为什么同一个问题两份拟合差这么多？核心在于 Kaplan et al. [Kaplan2020ScalingLaws] 的最优解是在固定 LR schedule + 相对较短训练步长下求的，LR 不随 D 重新 tune，导致大模型看起来 “数据够用就行”；Chinchilla 对每个 (N, D) 点都单独调 schedule，把 D 方向的梯度正确暴露出来。McCandlish et al. [McCandlish2018LargeBatch] 的 gradient noise scale 进一步解释了 critical batch size 随训练进行而增长——任何没有控制 batch schedule 的 scaling 拟合都会被这一机制污染。到了开源时代，DeepSeek LLM [DeepSeek2024LLM] 用独立数据与配置重拟合，发现 compute-optimal ratio 随数据 mixture 与 batch schedule 落在 5–100 tokens/param 的宽带里。把 Chinchilla 的 ≈ 20 当成宇宙常数是误读；它是特定数据、特定 schedule、特定 horizon 下的局部斜率。LLaMA [Touvron2023LLaMA] 事实上已经走得更远——主动 over-train 7B 到 1T tokens（ratio ≈ 143），换取部署期更低的 inference 成本，这是一条与 “compute-optimal” 完全不同但在生产上更合理的目标函数。6:T73c,In 2020 Kaplan et al. [Kaplan2020ScalingLaws] fit joint power laws for loss in N, D, C on WebText2 and concluded that at fixed compute the budget should go primarily to parameters — compute-optimal tokens/param ≈ 1.7. This shaped GPT-3 (175B / 300B tokens) and Gopher [Rae2021Gopher] (280B / 300B tokens). Two years later Hoffmann et al. [Hoffmann2022Chinchilla], using three mutually independent methods (IsoFLOP sweeps, parametric loss fit, learning-curve extrapolation), converged on tokens/param ≈ 20 — every frontier model of that era was under-trained by roughly an order of magnitude. Chinchilla-70B trained on 1.4T tokens beats Gopher-280B and GPT-3-175B, the most direct physical evidence of the corrected slope.

Why did two fits of the same problem diverge so strongly? Kaplan et al.'s [Kaplan2020ScalingLaws] optimum was obtained under a fixed LR schedule and short horizons; the LR was not re-tuned per D, so big models looked 'sufficiently trained' at modest D. Chinchilla re-tuned the schedule per (N, D) point, exposing the true D-direction gradient. McCandlish et al.'s [McCandlish2018LargeBatch] gradient noise scale further explains why the critical batch size grows over training — any scaling fit that fails to control batch schedule is silently polluted. In the open-source era, DeepSeek LLM [DeepSeek2024LLM] independently re-fit Chinchilla-style curves and found the compute-optimal ratio sliding across 5–100 tokens/param depending on data mixture and batch schedule. Reading Chinchilla's ≈ 20 as a universal constant is a misread; it is a local slope under specific data, schedule, and horizon. LLaMA [Touvron2023LLaMA] already went further — deliberately over-training 7B to 1T tokens (ratio ≈ 143) to cut inference cost. That is a different but operationally more sensible objective than 'compute-optimal'.7:T7be,Chinchilla 把数据送上 compute-optimal 曲线的同时，也埋下一个被随后两年反复挖出的假设：D 是一个一维标量，可以用 token 数代替。真实工程里 D 至少是 “token 数 × 质量 × 分布”。Li et al. [Li2024DCLM] 在 DCLM 上固定模型与 tokens 预算，只改数据筛选配方，下游平均就能拉开 ≥ 7 pp——这本身就证明 mixture 有自己的 scaling law。Gunasekar et al. [Gunasekar2023Textbooks] 的 phi-1 把这一点推到极端：1.3B 参数在代码基准上击败 10× 更大模型，是 “数据质量吞掉一条 N 轴” 的公开案例。Penedo et al. [Penedo2023RefinedWeb] 从另一头论证经过严格 dedup + filter 的 web-only 数据能匹敌 curated mixture——“The Pile 式多源混合” 不是默认答案。Engstrom et al. [Engstrom2024DsDm] 再戳一刀：人类审美式 “干净数据” 不一定最优，model-aware datamodels 选出的 “看起来脏” 子集在下游反而更强。换句话说，数据质量是 model-relative 的相对量，不是绝对属性。

在数据受限（data-wall）场景下，Muennighoff et al. [Muennighoff2023DataConstrained] 给出最重要的工程边界：≤ 4 epochs 内重复 tokens 与新鲜 tokens 近似等价，之后以可预测速率衰减。Hernandez et al. [Hernandez2022Repeated] 从微观上印证这一点——即便 2% 的数据被重复 100×，整体 loss 也会出现可测退化，并能在 induction head 激活上看到对应的能力塌陷。机制解释也顺理成章：induction head 容量有限，过多重复会把它消耗在记忆而不是泛化上。理论侧 Bahri et al. [Bahri2021Explaining] 的 data-manifold-dimension 解释与此一致——scaling 指数取决于任务在表征空间里的内在维度，mixture 变，指数就变。这些工作合起来给出一条务实原则：在 scaling plan 里把 D 永远当作 “tokens × mixture × 重复次数” 的三维量，而不是 tokens 一维。8:T7a8,Chinchilla put data on the compute-optimal curve but smuggled in an assumption later works spent two years undoing: that D is a one-dimensional scalar measurable in tokens. In practice D is at least 'tokens × quality × distribution'. Li et al. [Li2024DCLM] on DCLM hold N and token budget fixed, vary only the filtering recipe, and open ≥ 7 pp downstream average — proof that mixture has its own scaling law. Gunasekar et al.'s [Gunasekar2023Textbooks] phi-1 pushes the point to the extreme: 1.3B parameters beat 10× larger models on code, a public case of data quality swallowing an entire N axis. Penedo et al. [Penedo2023RefinedWeb] argue from the opposite end that aggressively deduped and filtered web-only data can match curated mixtures — the Pile-style multi-source default is not the answer. Engstrom et al. [Engstrom2024DsDm] land one more blow: human-aesthetic 'clean' data is not optimal; model-aware datamodel selection picks visually dirtier subsets that do better. Data quality is a model-relative quantity, not an absolute property.

In data-constrained regimes, Muennighoff et al. [Muennighoff2023DataConstrained] give the crucial engineering boundary: within ≈ 4 epochs, repeated tokens are near-equivalent to fresh ones; beyond, returns decay predictably. Hernandez et al. [Hernandez2022Repeated] confirm this mechanistically — even 2% of data repeated 100× produces measurable loss degradation with a matching dip in induction-head activations. The mechanism is clean: induction-head capacity is finite; excess repetition spends it on memorization rather than generalization. Theory (Bahri et al. [Bahri2021Explaining], via data-manifold dimension) agrees — the scaling exponent depends on the task's intrinsic dimension in representation space, so change the mixture and the exponent moves. The working principle: in any scaling plan, treat D as three-dimensional (tokens × mixture × repetition count), never as a scalar.9:T880,公司内 roadmap 里 “我们的模型会达到 MMLU X 分” 这种话几乎都默认一个跳跃：loss 外推 → task 分数外推。Gadre et al. [Gadre2024OverTraining] 用 104 个模型的 sweep 把这个假设拆开：loss 在 over-training regime 下依然服从可靠幂律（可外推到 ≈ 900 tokens/param），但单个下游 benchmark 的分数在训练过程中剧烈波动，要等到 loss 下降到某个任务特定阈值才稳定——把 loss 外推到 task 分数上必须经过一个非线性映射。Bhagia et al. [Bhagia2024TaskLadders] 把这件事系统化：先用 “模型阶梯” 拟合 task perplexity vs compute，再把 task perplexity 映射到 task accuracy，在 7 个 multiple-choice benchmark 上平均误差压到 1.9%。这是一条比直接拟合 accuracy 靠谱一个量级的流水线。

Isik et al. [Isik2024Downstream] 从 transfer 角度补枪：上游 loss 下降并不蕴含下游 BLEU / accuracy 的单调上升，尤其预训练分布与目标分布错配时，上游更好的模型 transfer 后反而更差。回头看 Hernandez et al. [Hernandez2021Transfer] 的 “effective transferred tokens” 其实已经预告了这件事——预训练在目标分布上能折算多少 token，是一个任务依赖的量。把三份工作摆在一起，结论很朴素：benchmark 数字不是 compute 的幂律函数，是 (compute, data, eval-metric) 的联合函数。

在这个背景下 Schaeffer et al. [Schaeffer2023Mirage] 的读法就不只是哲学反驳了。Wei et al. [Wei2022Emergent] 报告的多数 “突现能力” 在换成 token-level log-likelihood 后变成平滑曲线——这意味着很多 “N 越大才能做 X” 的说法本质是指标选择问题（exact match / 0-1 accuracy）。工程上的推论：在 scaling sweep 里用连续指标（perplexity、loss、log-likelihood）拟合，在发布里用离散指标报告，但不要把离散指标的台阶当作模型内部相变来规划 roadmap。Yuan et al. [Yuan2023Math] 的数学推理研究进一步给出微观证据——推理类任务的提升主要由预训练 loss 与监督数据量的乘积决定，而不是单独的 N 或 D。a:T808,Internal roadmaps saying 'our model will hit MMLU X' almost always smuggle in a jump: loss extrapolates → task score extrapolates. Gadre et al. [Gadre2024OverTraining] dismantle this via a 104-model sweep: loss follows a reliable power law even in heavy over-training (extrapolatable to ≈ 900 tokens/param), yet individual benchmarks oscillate wildly until loss drops below a task-specific threshold. Getting from loss to task score requires a nonlinearity. Bhagia et al. [Bhagia2024TaskLadders] operationalize this with a two-step regression: fit task perplexity vs compute via a compact model ladder, then map task perplexity to task accuracy, achieving 1.9% average error on 7 multiple-choice benchmarks — an order of magnitude more reliable than fitting accuracy directly.

Isik et al. [Isik2024Downstream] add a transfer-flavored correction: upstream loss decrease does not imply monotone downstream gains in BLEU or accuracy — under pretraining–target distribution mismatch, the upstream-better model can be downstream-worse. In hindsight, Hernandez et al.'s [Hernandez2021Transfer] 'effective transferred tokens' already warned of this: how many target-distribution tokens pretraining is worth is a task-dependent quantity. Putting these together: benchmark numbers are not a power law in compute; they are a joint function of (compute, data, eval metric).

Against this backdrop Schaeffer et al. [Schaeffer2023Mirage] stop being a philosophical jab. Most emergent abilities reported by Wei et al. [Wei2022Emergent] smooth out once you swap to token-level log-likelihood — meaning 'N must cross threshold T to do X' is often a metric artifact (exact match, 0-1 accuracy). Practical corollary: fit with continuous metrics in scaling sweeps, report discrete metrics in releases, but do not plan roadmaps as if the staircase were a real phase transition. (1) μP LR transfer sweep [Yang2022mup]：在 μP 参数化下最优 LR 在 width 方向可以零样本迁移，把 LR 从 big-model critical path 上拿掉。任何新架构、新数据 mixture 上先走一次 μP sweep，固定 LR 才能公平比较后续 N/D 方向的拟合。

A mature pretraining team runs three always-on sweeps that together constitute 'scaling laws as DevOps':

(1) μP LR transfer sweep [Yang2022mup]: under μP parametrization the optimal LR transfers zero-shot across widths, removing LR from the large-model critical path. On every new architecture or data mixture, run a μP sweep first; fix LR before comparing N/D fits.

(2) IsoFLOP token/param sweep: re-run a compact (N, D) grid on the new mixture to re-calibrate the local compute-optimal slope. DeepSeek LLM's [DeepSeek2024LLM] 5–100 tokens/param band is the warning — do not assume ≈ 20. Pair with Wortsman et al.'s [Wortsman2023SmallProxies] small-scale instability proxies to catch logit blow-ups at small scale before they pollute the fit.

(3) Data-mixture sweep: hold N, D fixed, vary filter and mixture, draw the data-space Pareto front. The open stack of DCLM [Li2024DCLM] + OLMo [Groeneveld2024OLMo] + Dolma [Soldaini2024Dolma] removes most of the scaffolding cost.

Published exponents are smell-test baselines, not production defaults. Anthropic's scaling-law team has consistently communicated (see [AnthropicScaling2023]) that the real value of a scaling law is not extrapolating a single number — it is quantifying 'how much compute a wrong decision costs you'. With your own loss-vs-compute curve, the rollback cost of a failed experiment is known before you launch it.
