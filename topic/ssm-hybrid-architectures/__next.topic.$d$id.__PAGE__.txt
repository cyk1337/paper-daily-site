A fixed-size hidden state cannot losslessly compress discrete, high-entropy information across long contexts. To bypass this bottleneck, the industry pivoted to Hybrid solutions in 2024: interleaving Attention and SSM layers at ratios between 1:3 and 1:7. This recovers associative recall while maintaining SSM's linear inference throughput. Jamba by Lieber et al. [Lieber2024Jamba], Samba by Ren et al. [Ren2024Samba], and Zamba by Glorioso et al. [Glorioso2024Zamba] represent the three validated Hybrid recipes. [Gu2023Mamba]: by making the A, B, and C matrices input-dependent (selective mechanism), Mamba matched Transformer pretraining loss at 7B for the first time.
